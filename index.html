Title: Software Validation Engineer for IoT Drone and Robotics
ID: 786830

Description
Successful candidate will have the opportunity to work in open source technology center of Intel, contributing to existing and future open source projects of core middleware systems on top of IoT operating systems and platforms tailored specifically for IoT systems with different capabilities, such as Flight Stacks, Robotics and Sensing/Vision, create an end-end solution to meet the IoT market requirements in Industrial Automation, AR/VR, Visual inspection and etc

The candidate will work as QA engineer, collaborate with PM, architect and co-work with developers to deliver high quality open source Linux-based IoT core middleware systems and capability stacks for IoT products. Be responsible for test plan definition, test methodologies, test case design, automation and test execution. He/she will also provide insightful quality data for product release.

Qualifications
Successful candidates will have the following qualifications:
- BS in CS or EE required, MS preferred
- 2+ years of experience in development of embedded systems.
- Familiar with Linux OS and expert in C, Python, Shell Script programing
- Familiar with test design and automation methodologies
- Excellent problem solving and debug ability
- Broad understanding of the Internet of Things and embedded technologies.
- Expert in one or multiple areas of drone, robotics, sensing/vision, and etc.
- Strong interpersonal skills and ability to work effectively with global engineering teams.
- Well organized and multitask ability. Proactive and insightful with a collaborative attitude.
- Good written and verbal communication skills with the ability to present deliverables and project impact.
- Strong self-initiative and persistence; ability to deal with a high degree of ambiguity and drive clarity in key areas.

Business Group
The Intel Software and Services Group (SSG) connects Intel to the worldwide software community. SSG strives to bring competitive advantage to Intel platforms by helping independent software vendors, operating system developers, OEMs, channel members and systems integrators deliver exceptional customer value and achieve differentiation on Intel® processor technologies. SSG provides global leadership to the software community through its technical expertise, industry enabling activities, and developer products and programs.
